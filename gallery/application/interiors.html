Norstone Rock Panels weigh about 12.5 lbs / sq ft, making most normal drywall or sheetrock wall substrates unsuitable for an interior rock wall installation. This is easily remedied by installating cement board over the existing wallboard and then installing the interior stone veneer over the freshly prepared substrate. Stone veneer interior walls have never been so easy! For more details on how to install stone veneer panels on interior walls check out our installation instructions.
